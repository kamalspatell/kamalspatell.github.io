About

Hi, I'm Kamal,

A graduate student in mechanical engineering, my interests lie in taking a product from design concept to production and everything in between. In my free time, I am either learning to code or out in the park running.

I interned at Instahub a small startup in Philidalphia, where I worked on mechanical design, prototyping, testing and validation of electro-mechanical product, SoLAR(Snap on Lightining Automation Retrofit Device). Working in a startup environment pushed me to constantly design and iterate at a quick pace and deliver results efficiently.

At graduate school I have also had an opportunity of working as a mechanical design engineer with an IGVC'18 Autonomous vehicle team and worked on projects related to soft robotics and have tutored undergraduate students in CAD design and 3D printing.
